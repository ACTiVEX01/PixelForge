Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

PixelForge ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at pixelforge-blog.vercel.app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Newsletter subscriptions: Your email address when you subscribe.
  • Comments: Your name, optional email address, and comment content.
  • Contact form: Your name, email, and message.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Log data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time and date of visit, and referring URL.
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies for site functionality and, if you consent, advertising cookies (Google AdSense).
  • Local storage: We use browser local storage to save your theme preference and article bookmarks. No personal data is stored there.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To send you our newsletter (only if you explicitly subscribed).
  • To display and moderate comments.
  • To respond to contact form enquiries.
  • To analyse site traffic and improve content.
  • To serve personalised advertisements through Google AdSense.

3. Google AdSense & Third-Party Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our site and other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet. For more information, see Google's advertising policies.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers: Hosting (Vercel, InfinityFree), database services — bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Google: For AdSense advertising and Analytics as described above.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights.

6. Data Retention

  • Newsletter emails are retained until you unsubscribe.
  • Comments are retained indefinitely unless you request deletion.
  • Contact form messages are retained for up to 12 months.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to access your personal data.
  • Right to correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to restrict or object to processing.
  • Right to data portability.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@pixelforge.blog.

8. Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. External Links

Our site may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
